Sales Call Question Bank

Click questions to mark them as asked. Use these to guide your conversations and uncover the right solution for each client.

🔍
Discovery Call
First contact — understand their world before pitching anything
Pain Points & Current Situation
  • What happens right now when a new lead comes in — who responds, and how fast?Opens the "slow response" pain point naturally
  • How many leads do you get per month right now, roughly?Feeds directly into the pricing calculator lead volume
  • What's your biggest bottleneck between getting a lead and closing a deal?
  • Do you have someone handling follow-ups, or do leads just go cold after the first touch?
  • What happens after hours or on weekends — are calls and messages still being answered?
  • Have you ever tried to estimate how much revenue you're losing from missed or slow responses?
Business Model & Scale
  • How many people are on your team right now?Determines size multiplier (small/medium/enterprise)
  • What's your average deal or service value?Feeds into ROI calculator
  • What tools or software are you currently using — CRM, booking, texting, anything like that?
  • Do you have an existing database of past leads or customers that aren't being worked?Opens the door for Database Reactivation service
  • Where do most of your leads come from — ads, website, referrals, social?
Goals & Readiness
  • If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about your sales process, what would it be?
  • Have you looked into AI or automation before? What was your experience?
  • What does your timeline look like — are you looking to make changes this month, or just exploring?
  • Is there a budget you've set aside for improving your sales infrastructure?Qualifies budget early — frame as investment, not cost
🎯
Pitch / Demo Call
Show the system — anchor value before revealing price
Value Anchoring
  • If you could respond to every lead within 60 seconds — 24/7 — how much more revenue do you think that would generate?Gets the prospect to self-quantify the value
  • What would it cost to hire someone full-time to do what this AI does — answer calls, send follow-ups, book appointments?Anchors against $3,500-5,000/mo for a full-time employee
  • How many extra deals per month would it take for this to pay for itself?Usually 1-2 deals — makes the ROI obvious
  • Right now, what percentage of your leads would you say actually get proper follow-up?
Objection Discovery
  • What's your biggest concern about using AI in your business?Surfaces objections early so you can address them
  • Is there anyone else involved in this decision that we should loop in?Avoids "I need to talk to my partner" later
  • What would need to be true for you to feel confident moving forward with this?
  • Have you been burned by an agency or automation tool before? What happened?Builds trust — shows you care about their past experience
Fit & Scope
  • Of the services I showed you — chatbot, voice AI, SMS, follow-ups, booking — which ones would make the biggest impact for you?Helps build a custom package vs. overwhelming them
  • Would you want to start with the core package and expand later, or go all-in from day one?Sets up Starter vs Flagship bundle decision
  • Are there any specific integrations you'd need — calendar, CRM, phone system, POS?
📈
Proposal / Close Call
Present the quote — handle objections — close the deal
Pre-Close Temperature Check
  • Since our last call, have you had any more thoughts or questions about the system?
  • On a scale of 1-10, how interested are you in getting this set up?Anything below 7 = address concerns before presenting price
  • Has anything changed in your business since we last spoke that I should know about?
Price Presentation & Framing
  • Before I go through the numbers — would you rather see the full system or the starter package?Lets them self-select budget level
  • Would quarterly or annual billing work better for you? There's a discount for committing longer.Frames discount as a reward, not a concession
  • Based on what you told me — [X leads/mo] at [$X deal value] — the system only needs to close [1-2] extra deals per month to pay for itself. Does that math make sense to you?Tie back to their own numbers from discovery
Objection Handling
  • Is it the price that's giving you pause, or is there something else?Separates real objections from price anchoring
  • If the price wasn't a factor, would you move forward today?If yes = price objection. If no = dig deeper.
  • What would you need to see in the first 30 days to feel like this was a great investment?Sets realistic expectations and success criteria
  • You mentioned [concern] — let me show you exactly how we handle that.Reference a specific concern from earlier in the convo
Closing
  • Everything we build is installed in your own tools — you own it. The only question is: do you want us to get started this week or next?Assumptive close — emphasizes ownership
  • We can have your system live in about a week. What day works to kick off?Moves from "if" to "when"
  • Would you like to start with the starter bundle and upgrade later, or lock in the full system now at the better rate?Gives two "yes" options instead of yes/no
Current & Returning Clients
  • How has the system been performing for you so far? What's working best?Opens upsell opportunity from a place of success
  • Are there any channels your leads are coming from that the AI isn't covering yet?Identifies add-on opportunities (WhatsApp, social DM, email)
  • Have your lead volumes changed? We should make sure your system is scaled to match.Justifies tier/pricing adjustments for growing clients
  • Would you be interested in owning the system outright? We have a licensing option that could save you a lot long-term.Opens the white-label / licensing conversation
